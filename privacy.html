Privacy Policy

This site (tvlogos.austheim.app) serves static TV channel logo images and a JSON index of them. Here's what that does and doesn't involve.

What we collect

Standard web server logs (IP address, requested file, timestamp, user agent) are generated automatically by the hosting infrastructure for operational purposes — diagnosing errors, understanding traffic, and preventing abuse. We do not run user accounts, do not require sign-in, and do not collect names, emails, or other personal information through this site.

Cookies and analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, general geography, referring sites). Google Analytics may set cookies in your browser as part of that. You can opt out using your browser's cookie settings or a tool like the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Third-party requests

Logo images and the manifest are served with permissive CORS headers so other applications (IPTV players, EPG tools, media centers) can fetch them directly. If you're viewing this site's assets embedded in another app, that app's own privacy policy governs what it does with the data — this site has no visibility into that.

Advertising

This site may display third-party ads in the future. If enabled, the ad provider (e.g. Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your visit to this and other sites. You can learn more and manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the logo request form linked from the homepage.
